Founder's House Coffee Shop

About Us:

Founder’s House Coffee Shop serves coffee with a mission. Built by startup founders, for startup founders, the Founder’s HAouse provides an excellent place to grab a cup of delicious coffee and co-work or socialize with other startup founders. Additionally, for every purchase made, Founder’s House will round up to the nearest dollar and make a donation to the Pharos Founder’s Collective in support of entrepreneurial mental health.
